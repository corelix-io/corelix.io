Frequently asked questions

Can I start with the free edition and upgrade later?

Yes, seamlessly. Your settings and data are preserved. The Pro license unlocks additional features on your existing installation without migration or data loss.

Do I need Coolify to use Corelix?

Yes, Corelix extends Coolify v4. It installs as a drop-in addon using Coolify's native custom compose support — no source modifications, no forks.

Where is my data stored?

Self-hosted: wherever you deploy. You control the servers, the network, and the data. Managed Cloud: EU data centers in France and Germany, with no data transfer outside the European Economic Area.

Is Corelix GDPR compliant?

Yes. Self-hosted gives you full control as both data controller and processor. Managed Cloud processes data exclusively in the EU with no US subsidiary exposure — eliminating the CLOUD Act risk that affects US-headquartered providers.

What happens if I uninstall Corelix?

Coolify continues working normally. Your projects, applications, and databases are untouched. Corelix-specific settings are preserved in the database. Note: encrypted backups will need the original password to restore.

How does pricing compare to Vercel or Railway?

A €4/mo Hetzner VPS + Corelix Free gives you more features than Vercel's $20/mo Pro plan — including encrypted backups, network isolation, granular permissions, and 72 MCP tools — with full EU data residency and no per-seat charges.

Is there a free trial for Pro?

Yes, 14 days free. No credit card required. You get full access to all Pro features including cluster management, whitelabeling, and Docker registry management.
