Frequently asked questions

Can I start with Hobby and upgrade?

Yes, seamlessly. Upgrade to Pro or Team at any time. Your apps, databases, and settings are preserved.

Do I need to manage servers?

No. Corelix provisions and manages EU servers for you. You just deploy your code.

Where is my data stored?

All managed servers are in EU data centers (Hetzner, Germany and Finland). Your data never leaves the European Economic Area.

Is Corelix GDPR compliant?

Yes. All infrastructure is EU-based with no US subsidiary exposure, eliminating CLOUD Act risk entirely.

What happens if I want to leave?

No vendor lock-in. Export your data anytime. Our open-source self-hosted tools at github.com/amirhmoradi/coolify-enhanced let you run the same platform independently.

How does pricing compare to Vercel?

A Corelix Pro plan with one server (~€31/mo total) gives you more features than Vercel's $20/mo Pro plan — including databases, backups, network isolation, and full EU data residency.

Can I bring my own servers?

Yes, on Team and Enterprise plans. Connect any server via SSH alongside managed ones.

Prefer to self-host? Our open-source community tools let you run the platform on your own infrastructure.

Still have questions?

Our team is happy to walk you through the right plan for your infrastructure needs.
